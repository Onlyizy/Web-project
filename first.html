My Education so far

Prestigious Military Academy of Bingerville

I was blessed with a great high-school experience in one of the most prestigious schools of my native country Ivory Coast.
The Preparatory and Technical Military School of Bingerville (Ecole Militare Preparatoire et Technique or EMPT de Bingerville ) is a school teaching its students discipline and hardwork since. Providing them with all the tools needed to navigate the world around them and inculquing them true values. Since its creation the school raised plenty of legends like the former Commander in chief the General Soumaila Bakayoko , The former Guinean President Lansana Conté, The Ivorian minister Sidi Tiémoko Touré

Carleton University

Travelling and discovering the world is, to me, one of the best ways to learn.
I came in Canada already eager to learn about Computers, not only the software nor only the hardware. I had a clear idea of what I wanted, all I needed was the Program and the University proposing the program. Carleton University was a great choice to me as they had a great engineering department and had a Computer System Engineering Program.
